A simple, guided walkthrough of what happens from the moment you sign up to your first month of results — no surprises, just a clear path.

📋
Step 1

Before You Start

Instant

You choose Starter Growth and book your free 20-minute Growth Audit. That's it — no commitment, just a conversation about your business.

🤝
Step 2 · Day 0–2

Onboarding

1–2 business days

We confirm your package and set up a short kickoff call to understand your business and collect the basics we need to get started.

What you receive
  • Instant SMS & email confirmation
  • A 15–20 minute kickoff call
🔍
Step 3 · Week 1

Growth Audit

2–3 days

We review how leads currently reach you, what social channels you use, and how follow-up happens today — then share a short summary of what we found.

What you receive
  • A one-page audit summary
  • Clear next steps for your system
📐
Step 4 · Week 1–2

System Design

3–5 days

We design a simple, connected setup: one form or landing page, one follow-up flow, and the scope of your AI chat agent.

What you receive
  • A simple visual plan of your system
  • Agreed scope for your AI chat agent
⚙️
Step 5 · Week 2–3

Build & Setup

5–7 days

We build everything behind the scenes — your CRM, your form, your AI chat agent, your follow-up automation, and your first content batch.

What's being built
  • Basic CRM setup
  • Form or landing page
  • Basic AI chat agent
  • Initial follow-up automation
  • First content batch drafted
🚀
Step 6 · Week 3–4

Launch

2–3 days

We test the full flow together — lead comes in, CRM captures it, automation follows up — and confirm everything works before going live.

What you receive
  • CRM access
  • Live AI chat agent
  • A guided end-to-end test
📅
Step 7 · Ongoing

Month 1 & Beyond

Every month

Your system keeps running — content gets posted, follow-up keeps happening, and your AI agent keeps answering questions, with basic support along the way.

What you receive every month
  • 8 monthly social posts
  • Content scheduling
  • Ongoing follow-up automation
  • Basic support for adjustments
